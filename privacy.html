IMPORTANT SAFETY NOTICE

SafeCallNow is a personal safety support tool and is not an emergency service.

If you are in immediate danger, require emergency assistance, or believe a crime is being committed, call 911 or your local emergency services provider immediately.

SafeCallNow does not contact emergency services, law enforcement, security personnel, family members, or other third parties on a user's behalf.

The app does not guarantee personal safety, prevent criminal activity, provide emergency intervention, or monitor users in real time. Users should not rely on the app as their sole safety measure.

1. Introduction

SafeCallNow ("SafeCallNow," "we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting information relating to users of our mobile application (the "app").

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, processed, used, disclosed, stored, and retained when you access or use the app.

SafeCallNow utilizes artificial intelligence technology to simulate natural, real-time voice conversations in a variety of languages, accents, and dialects. The app is designed to provide users with an additional sense of comfort, confidence, or support in situations where they may feel vulnerable, uncomfortable, isolated, or unsafe.

By downloading, accessing, or using the app, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

2. No Account Required

SafeCallNow does not require users to create an account or register to use the app.

We do not maintain user profiles and do not intentionally collect information that directly identifies individual users.

3. Information We Collect

SafeCallNow is designed to operate with minimal collection of information.

We do not intentionally collect, store, sell, rent, license, or otherwise process personal information such as:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Telephone numbers
  • Mailing addresses
  • Government-issued identification numbers
  • Payment information
  • Contact lists
  • Photographs
  • User communications
  • Precise location information

Because SafeCallNow does not require registration, we generally cannot identify individual users.

For information about how voice audio is handled during AI voice calls, see Section 5 (Artificial Intelligence Functionality).

Certain limited technical information may be generated automatically when the app communicates with hosting, security, content delivery, and infrastructure providers.

This information may include:

  • Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Device type
  • Operating system and version
  • Device language settings
  • Date and time of access
  • Network performance data
  • Diagnostic information
  • Security logs
  • Service availability metrics

Such information is processed solely for legitimate operational purposes, including:

  • Delivering app functionality
  • Maintaining service reliability
  • Monitoring system performance
  • Troubleshooting technical issues
  • Preventing abuse and unauthorized access
  • Protecting the security and integrity of the App

We do not use this information to identify users, build user profiles, conduct behavioral advertising, or track users across third-party applications or websites.

5. Artificial Intelligence Functionality

SafeCallNow utilizes artificial intelligence systems to generate simulated conversational interactions.

During an AI voice call, audio from your device's microphone is transmitted in real time to third-party artificial intelligence service providers for the sole purpose of generating the conversational responses you hear. SafeCallNow does not record, store, or retain this audio, and does not use it to identify you. These providers process the audio in real time to deliver the service and may retain limited data for a short period only as necessary to operate the service and to maintain its security and integrity. This audio is not used to identify you and is not used for advertising.

The content generated by the app is automated and may vary based on user inputs, device conditions, language selection, and system performance.

AI-generated content is provided solely for simulation and support purposes and should not be interpreted as:

  • Professional advice
  • Legal advice
  • Medical advice
  • Mental health advice
  • Security advice
  • Emergency assistance

The AI system does not make decisions on behalf of users and does not replace personal judgment or emergency services.
