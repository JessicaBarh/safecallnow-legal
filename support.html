Frequently asked questions

What is SafeCall Now?

A companion app that puts a friendly presence on the line whenever you want one — a real-time voice call with Riley, our AI companion, plus a library of realistic practice video calls.

How do I talk to Riley?

Open the Feed tab and tap the "Talk to Riley" card. Grant microphone access when prompted, and you'll be connected within a few seconds. You can mute, switch to speaker, or end the call at any time.

Are my calls recorded?

No. Voice calls are streamed in real time to generate Riley's responses and are not recorded or stored by SafeCall Now. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Do I need an account?

No. There's no sign-up or login — just open the app and go.

What languages are supported?

The practice conversation videos are available in multiple languages, selectable from the Languages tab.

How do I report a problem or request a feature?

Email us at safecallnowapp@gmail.com with a description and, if possible, your device model and iOS version.
